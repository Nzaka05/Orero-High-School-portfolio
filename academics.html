Sciences at Orero

We offer hands-on lab experiences, research projects, and national science fair participation in Physics, Chemistry & Biology. Labs are fully equipped.

Technology

Students learn core computing skills, coding basics, and internet safety. Computer Studies is fully integrated with 40+ machines available.

Engineering

Through Technical Drawing and practical skills, students explore the basics of engineering logic and design thinking.

Mathematics

Our math department focuses on logical thinking, competitive exams like Kangaroo, and real-world applications of math concepts.

Languages

Emphasis on fluency, critical thinking through literature, and language clubs like Debate & Journalism. Optional French available.

Humanities

Students explore civic responsibility, world history, and geography through engaging lessons, debates, and community projects.

Creative Arts

We nurture talent through music, drama and art festivals. Students develop confidence and expression through creative disciplines.
