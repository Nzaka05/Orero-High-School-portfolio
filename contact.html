Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about contacting and visiting our school

Located approximately 20km from Homa Bay Town. Also reachable from Kisii via Oyugis. You can take public transport (matatus) or private vehicles. Look for signage indicating "Orero Secondary School" along the highwayor on arrival at Rodi Kopany. For detailed directions, please call our office.

The best times to visit are weekdays between 8:00 AM and 4:00 PM when staff are available. For tours or meetings with the principal, please schedule an appointment in advance. Avoid visiting during examination periods unless it's urgent.

We typically respond to emails within 24 hours during weekdays. Phone calls are answered immediately during office hours. For urgent matters, please call our main line. WhatsApp messages are usually responded to within a few hours.

For all admission-related inquiries, contact our Admissions Office at admissions@orerohigh.ac.ke or call +254722288230. The admissions team can provide information about the application process, requirements, and deadlines.

Yes, we have a designated parking area for visitors near the main administration block. Please report to the security gate upon arrival for directions to the parking area and visitor registration.
