Admission Statement

Admissions to Orero High School are handled through Kenya's Ministry of Education Form 1 Placement system. Students are selected based on their KCPE performance and school preference choices.

For New Students (Form 1)

  • Complete KCPE examination
  • List Orero High School as your preference
  • Wait for MOE placement notification
  • Report on the specified date

For Transfer Students

  • Contact the school directly
  • Email: admissions@orerohigh.ac.ke
  • Visit the school office
  • Office hours: 8:00 AM - 4:00 PM
