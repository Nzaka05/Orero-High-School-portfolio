Featured News

Featured January 9, 2024

Orero High School Achieves Outstanding KCSE Results

Our students have once again demonstrated excellence with a mean score of 9.4235 in the 2024 KCSE examinations. This remarkable achievement places us among the top 10% of schools in the county, with 100% of our graduates qualifying for university admission. The results reflect our unwavering commitment to academic excellence and the dedication of our teaching staff.
